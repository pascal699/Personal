About Me

I'm a young innovator from Rwanda passionate about building practical devices using local materials. I believe innovation doesn't need expensive tools—just creativity, purpose, and the will to solve real-life problems.

Projects

  • 🔬 Built a functioning compound microscope using wood, plastic lenses, and LED lights
  • ❄️ Created a refrigerator model powered by local thermal techniques and recyclable containers
  • 🎓 Presented innovations at school exhibitions to inspire other students
  • ⚙️ Focused on affordable, scalable tech for rural communities

What Drives Me

I’m driven by the dream to make science practical and useful for every Rwandan student. Through my projects, I want to promote hands-on learning and inspire youth to see that they too can innovate with whatever they have.

Barça Spirit

Besides building things, I’m a huge fan of FC Barcelona. I admire the team's strategy, resilience, and team spirit—which I apply to my life and work as well.
